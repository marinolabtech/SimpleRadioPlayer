# Privacy Policy for SimpleRadioPlayer

**Last updated:** July 2026

**MARINOLAB** ("we", "our", or "us") operates and maintains the **SimpleRadioPlayer** mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use our application.

We respect your privacy: **SimpleRadioPlayer does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII) to external servers operated by MARINOLAB.**

---

## 1. Information Collection and Use

### A. Personal Data
SimpleRadioPlayer **does not require or collect** any personal identifiable information, such as your name, email address, phone number, exact precise location, or contacts. No account creation or registration is required to use the app.

### B. Local Data Storage (On Device Only)
To provide core functionality, the app stores data locally on your device:
* **Favorites List:** Your saved favorite radio stations and custom display order.
* **App Settings:** User preferences (e.g., Car Mode state, playback settings).

> **Note:** None of this local data ever leaves your device or gets synchronized with external servers.

---

## 2. App Permissions and Purpose

SimpleRadioPlayer requests specific Android system permissions strictly necessary for its technical operations:

* **`INTERNET` & `ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE`:** Required to connect to web streams provided by radio stations, perform station searches, and monitor connection stability (buffer/bitrate).
* **`FOREGROUND_SERVICE` & `FOREGROUND_SERVICE_MEDIA_PLAYBACK`:** Required to ensure continuous audio playback in the background or when the screen is locked, displaying media playback controls in the notification area.
* **`WAKE_LOCK` / Power State:** Used in Car Mode to detect whether the device is connected to a power source, keeping the display active while driving.

---

## 3. Third-Party Connections and Libraries

### A. Radio Station Streams
When you tune into a radio station, your device connects directly to that third-party station's streaming server. Like any standard internet connection, the destination server may log your IP address for streaming delivery or aggregate listening statistics. These connections are governed by the privacy policies of the respective radio broadcasters.

### B. Third-Party Libraries Used
The app utilizes open-source libraries to ensure optimal performance:
* **Jetpack Media3 / ExoPlayer (Google):** Handles media playback.
* **OkHttp:** Manages network requests.
* **Glide:** Displays station logos and images.

Our application is not directed toward children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
